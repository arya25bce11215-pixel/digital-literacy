1. Maintain a Professional Profile
Keep your bio, profile picture, and posts clean and appropriate. This helps build a positive impression for internships and placements.
 2. Network Smartly
Connect with classmates, seniors, professors, and professionals. Platforms like LinkedIn are great for career growth.
 3. Share Useful Content
Post about your projects, achievements, certifications, and learning experiences. It builds your personal brand.
 4. Protect Your Privacy
Adjust privacy settings and avoid sharing personal details like address, phone number, or sensitive information.
 5. Think Before You Post
Always ask yourself: “Will this affect my future?” Post responsibly and respectfully.
 Social Media Don’ts
1. Don’t Overshare Personal Information
Avoid posting your location, daily routine, or private life details that can risk your safety.
 2. Don’t Post Offensive Content
Stay away from controversial, abusive, or inappropriate posts that can harm your reputation.
 3. Don’t Waste Too Much Time
Avoid excessive scrolling on apps like Instagram or TikTok, which can affect your productivity.
 4. Don’t Post Unprofessional Photos
Avoid uploading pictures that may create a negative impression in academic or professional settings.
 5. Don’t Engage in Online Arguments
Avoid unnecessary debates or fights in comments. It can damage your digital image.
